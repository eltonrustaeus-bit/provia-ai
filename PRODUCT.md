# ProviaAI — Product Context

## Product Purpose
AI-powered exam training platform for Swedish students. Students paste study material → the platform generates mock exams → AI grades answers with detailed feedback and model solutions. Also includes a dedicated Swedish driver's license theory module (körkort) with 350 curated questions.

## Register
product

## Users
Swedish high school and university students preparing for exams. Age 16–25. Also Swedish residents studying for the driver's license theory test (körkortsprovet). Tech-comfortable but not developers. Mobile use is common — studying on the bus, in short sessions.

## Brand Tone
Sharp, focused, trustworthy. Feels like a smart tool, not a toy. No excessive friendliness or emojis. The AI capability should feel precise and credible, not gimmicky. Swedish language throughout. Slightly premium — students are paying or considering paying.

## Pages
- **index.html** — Landing page. Hero, features, how-it-works, social proof, CTA.
- **app.html** — Core app: material input, exam generation, AI grading, results.
- **korkortet.html** — Driver's license theory quiz. 350 questions with images, categories, progress tracking.
- **pricing.html** — Gratis / Basic / Premium plans.
- **förbättring.html** — User feedback/improvement suggestions form.
- **admin.html** — Admin panel (internal only).

## Strategic Principles
1. Speed and focus over decoration — students have limited time.
2. Results and feedback are the core value; everything else is scaffolding.
3. The green accent (#1bff8c) signals AI activity and correct answers — use it consistently, not decoratively.
4. Mobile-first interactions for the quiz UI.

## Anti-References
- Duolingo (gamified, playful, notification-heavy) — we are not gamified
- Generic SaaS dashboards with blue/white/purple — we have a distinct dark green identity
- Overloaded feature panels — clarity over completeness
